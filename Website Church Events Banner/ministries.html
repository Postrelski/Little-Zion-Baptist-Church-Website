Ministry Overview

“The Church That Cares For You” (1 Peter 5:7)

Core Values

  • God loves lost people and so do we.
  • God’s Word shared with God’s power transforms lives.
  • Minister to the wounded and the broken and share the hope of the gospel that Jesus heals hurts and brings wholeness to life.
  • Seeking to minister to the total person, we will be innovative in our approach of spreading the gospel without compromising the Word of God.
  • Every Christian is uniquely gifted by God and called to serve with excellence according to their giftedness.

Commitments & Focus

  • Operate professionally and efficiently.
  • Have competent, responsible people building a strong team.
  • Provide the best servant leadership programs that foster care to our community and abroad.
  • Support our members and partners to achieve their potential.
  • Being accountable for every word or action.
  • Treating each other with dignity and respect.
  • Being the embodiment of Christ and serving as the model for future generations to follow.
  • Encouraging personal growth by developing and empowering our people.
  • Improving the organization by improving ourselves.

Ministerial Goals

  • Reach out to those in need of a Savior’s love (Mark 16:15)
  • Grow disciples through the Word of God (Ephesians 4:11-16)
  • Build up spiritual and community leaders (1 Peter 2:9)
  • Outreach local and foreign missions (Deuteronomy 10:18, Matthew 25:35)
  • Establish an atmosphere of worship and praise (Psalm 150:6, John 4:24)
  • Build a community based on love and servant leadership (Philippians 2:3-8)
  • Honor those who came before us and inspire those behind us (Psalms 78:5-8, 1 Peter 5:8)
